Portrait With Attitude

Commissioned portrait

Gorillaz-themed portrait. This portrait was made as a reference to the commissioner's view on New York City, where he grew up. With the abundance of people but the lack of time, many walk around wearing a "mask". Everybody accepts this as the wearers true identity. The "W" hand-sign represents "Wednesday", the commissioner's nickname.
